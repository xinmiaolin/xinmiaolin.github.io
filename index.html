About Me

I am a Ph.D. student (2020-) at B. Thomas Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences (GCCIS) of Rochester Institute of Technology (R.I.T.), working with Dr. Yu Kong. Prior to RIT, I received my Master's degree (2018-2020) at the Manning College of Information & Computer Sciences of University of Massachusetts Amherst, and my bachelor's degree at the School of Computer Science of McGill University. .

My research focus is on model explanation for computer vision models. My research experience include model explanation for action recognition models, image recognition models and adversarial machine learning.
